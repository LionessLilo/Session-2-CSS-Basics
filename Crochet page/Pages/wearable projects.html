Waterlily Lace Top

Product Details

Do not be fooled by how intricate the lace in this crochet top looks, it is actually easy! So easy that I like to call it “TV watching lace” because, once you get the set up round done, there is no counting required! In this crochet top pattern, you will learn how to make this country chic lace crochet top. Included is the written pattern with size instructions from XS to 2XL and there is also a helpful video tutorial!
